Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is AASO Pharmaceuticals?

AASO Pharmaceuticals is a leading provider of pharmaceutical products, committed to delivering high-quality healthcare solutions across various therapeutic areas.

2. How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team by emailing us at aasopharmaceuticals@gmail.com or calling us at 889-063-1560.

3. What products do you offer?

We offer a wide range of pharmaceutical products, including prescription medications, over-the-counter drugs, and healthcare supplements. Please visit our Products page for more details.

4. How do I place an order?

Orders can be placed directly through our website or by contacting our sales team. For more information, please refer to the Ordering section of our website.

5. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary based on the destination. Please contact us for more details.

6. How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, we will provide you with a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your order on our website or the carrier's website.

7. What is your return policy?

We accept returns for products that are unopened and in their original packaging within 30 days of purchase. Please refer to our Return Policy page for more details.

8. How can I stay updated on new products and promotions?

You can stay updated by subscribing to our newsletter or following us on social media. We regularly post updates about new products, promotions, and other important information.

9. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, and UPI. For more information, please visit our Payment Methods page.

10. Can I cancel or modify my order?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your order before it has been shipped. Please contact our customer support team as soon as possible to make any changes.
